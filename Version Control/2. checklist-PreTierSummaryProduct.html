Step 1: Set Up Your Tiers

Click on ⚙️ Configuration Management above to expand it, then click ➕ Add Tier to create your first tier. Give it a name (e.g., "Tier 1: Fruit Stand"), choose an icon (emoji), and select a color.

Step 2: Add Businesses

In the Configuration Management panel, click ➕ Add Business to add businesses to your tiers. Fill in:

  • Business Code: A unique identifier (e.g., "TIER1-BIZ001")
  • Business Name: The location or name (e.g., "Route 68, Grand Senora Desert")
  • Tier: Select which tier this business belongs to
  • Max Stock: Maximum stock capacity for this business
  • Collection Storage: Maximum collection storage capacity
  • Notes: Optional - Add notes to help remember which business this is, such as employee names or other identifying information. Hover over the ℹ️ icon for more details. This makes it easy to identify businesses when you have multiple locations.
Step 3: Track Your Progress

Once businesses are added, they will appear in the checklist below. You can:

  • Check off businesses: Click the checkbox when you've completed a task
  • Add Money: Enter the current money amount in the Money field
  • Track Stock: Enter current stock to see how much more is needed to reach max
  • Track Collection: Enter collection storage amounts (if applicable)
  • Add Notes: Add any notes or reminders for each business
Step 4: Import/Export Configuration

You can backup and restore your configuration:

  • Export: Click 📤 Export Configuration to copy your setup. Then copy the JSON text:
    • Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + A then Ctrl + C
    • Mac: Press Cmd + A then Cmd + C
  • Import: Click 📥 Import Configuration, then paste your saved JSON:
    • Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + V
    • Mac: Press Cmd + V
  • Click Import to load your configuration

All your progress is automatically saved to your browser. Use 📋 Generate Checklist to create a CSV report. For more details on copy/paste shortcuts, see the Traveling Merchants page.
