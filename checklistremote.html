Step 1: Build Your Run List

Type a business code or name in the search box. Click a result to add it to your run list. Only open businesses appear.

Step 2: Set Your Order

Click 🔀 Reorder to reveal drag handles on each card. Drag cards to your preferred run order, then click 🔀 Reorder again to lock it in. Your order is saved automatically.

Step 3: Track Your Progress

For each business you can:

  • Check the checkbox to mark it as completed
  • Enter Money collected from the business
  • Enter Current Stock to calculate how much stock is needed
  • Enter Collection amount (if the business has collection storage)
  • Select a Product to track inventory needs
  • Add Notes for any extra info
Step 4: Manage Your List
  • Click ❌ on a card to remove it from your run list
  • Click 👁️ on a card to hide it temporarily without removing it
  • Use the ⚡ FAB menu to Check All, Uncheck All, Clear Products, or Clear Progress
  • Progress is auto-saved as you type

💡 Need to configure tiers, businesses, or products? Do that first on the 📋 Business Checklist — your data syncs automatically to this page.
