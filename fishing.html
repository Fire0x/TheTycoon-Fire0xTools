üêü Managing Fish
Step 1: Select a Location

Step 2: Add Individual Fish

Click ‚ûï Add Fish to manually add a fish. Fill in:

üèÜ Managing Fishing Rewards
Step 1: Add a Reward

Click ‚ûï Add Reward and fill in:

  • Reward Name - Name of the reward

Step 2: View All Rewards

All rewards are displayed in the Rewards tab, sorted by level requirement. Claimed rewards are marked with a badge and appear faded.
