Traveling Merchants

Track traveling merchants, their rotations, locations, prices, and timers.

⚠️
Backup Reminder: Keep a regular backup of your .json file and store it somewhere safe. Browser updates or clearing site data can wipe localStorage — this is outside my control, though I do my best to prevent it. A simple way to keep your data safe is to create a private repository on GitHub. Need help? Here is a quick guide to get started. — Thank you, Fire0x

Step 1: In-Game

Open your chat and type the command:

/merchants
Step 2: Copy the Data

Select all the merchant data that appears:

  • Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + A then Ctrl + C
  • Mac: Press Cmd + A then Cmd + C
Step 3: Paste on This Page

Click in the text area below and paste your data:

  • Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + V
  • Mac: Press Cmd + V
Step 4: Save

Click the Parse & Save Merchants button to add them to your tracker!
